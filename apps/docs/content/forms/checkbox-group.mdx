Group of checkboxes for multi-select options with label and description. Wraps multiple <ComponentLink name="Checkbox" /> components with shared layout and labeling.

### Quick tips

- Pass `items` to render a group of checkboxes with consistent spacing.
- Use `direction` to switch between `vertical` (default) and `horizontal` layout.
- Supports `label` and `description` props for the group as a whole.
- Control selected values with `value` and `onValueChange`.
