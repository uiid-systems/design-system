Group of radio buttons for single-select options with label and description. Wraps multiple <ComponentLink name="Radio" /> components with shared layout and labeling.

### Quick tips

- Pass `items` to render a group of radio buttons with consistent spacing.
- Use `direction` to switch between `vertical` (default) and `horizontal` layout.
- Supports `label` and `description` props for the group as a whole.
- Control selected value with `value` and `onValueChange`.
